BloodBridge

Connecting blood donors and seekers BloodBridge is an innovative digital platform designed to connect blood donors with recipients in real-time. With a mission to save lives by simplifying access to blood, BloodBridge serves as a vital link between those in urgent need and compassionate donors nearby. Users can request or donate blood through an intuitive interface, access verified donor profiles, receive live updates, and find nearby blood banks or hospitals.
