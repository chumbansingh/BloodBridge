Frequently
Asked Questions

How do I become a donor?

Just register an account and fill out the donor form under the “Donate” section. You’ll be listed for matching in nearby requests.

Is Blood Bridge free to use?

Yes, the platform is completely free for both donors and requesters. Our goal is to build a life-saving community.

Can I track my donations?

Yes! Once registered, your donation history is saved to your profile, and you get reminders when you're eligible to donate again.

How do I request blood?

Click on the “Request” tab, fill out the required details including location and blood type, and submit the request instantly.

How are donors verified?

We verify donors via contact verification and medical history questions to ensure safety and reliability of all donations.

Can hospitals use this platform?

Absolutely! Hospitals can register to directly request and manage blood supplies through our system with real-time access.
