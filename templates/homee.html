Why Blood Bridge?

In Nepal, the existing blood management system is outdated and inefficient, relying heavily on manual record-keeping and delayed processes.

Blood Bridge is a digital platform designed to revolutionize this system by offering a real-time, smart, and transparent solution from donor registration to hospital coordination.

We reduce paperwork, improve emergency response, and ensure availability through verified data and community-driven support.

Because in times of crisis, the right information at the right moment can save a life.
